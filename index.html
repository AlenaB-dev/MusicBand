Hello and thank you for dropping by. My name is Henrik and I wrote some songs, so I decided to link up with my friend Ash, whom I have known for at least 10 years. He is a fantastic multi- instrumentalist and music teacher. Our 2-piece duo is called Rygvind (Danish for wind in the back).

We have decided to put our debut single Shorter Days out there for now, but there is more to come very soon. We hope somebody may pick it up and record it professionally one day. It is our long time aim to write songs for others, while Ash and I just sing and play occasionally for fun in local pubs here in Buckinghamshire. We are based in Hazlemere and Wooburn Green in Buckinghamshire.

Music recorded in Backgarden studios Wooburn Green.
